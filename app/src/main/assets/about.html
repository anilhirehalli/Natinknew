About MLH

Major League Hacking (MLH) is the official student hackathon league. Each year, They power over 200 weekend-long invention competitions that inspire innovation, cultivate communities and teach computer science skills to more than 65,000 students around the world. MLH is an engaged and passionate maker community, consisting of the next generation of technology leaders and entrepreneurs. Their Mission is to Empower Hackers

About NatInk

Nat Ink is an app which provides S.O.S service app which can be used during an emergency or causality. Causality can be Accidents and so on. If the user of the smartphone is unconscious then any person can activate the S.O.S by pressing a send Emergency Message .
